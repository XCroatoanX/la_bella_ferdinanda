About Us

We are a small hobby cattery of Maine Coon cats. Since childhood, I have grown up surrounded by animals. There were many cats and dogs nearby, all of them being strays from the streets. Therefore, I had to take care of them and feed them in homemade cardboard houses or in the basements of houses. Unfortunately, this was the reality of the recently collapsed Soviet Union. There were many homeless, unwanted animals.

I later wanted to become a veterinarian. But by fate, I first completed training as a nurse. Then I went on to study biology at the Saint Petersburg State University.

Throughout my life, animals, especially cats, have been my companions. My love for them not only did not fade, but grew into my favorite hobby of breeding and selecting these animals.

I started with the Persian breed of cats. For almost 10 years, I was involved in breeding this breed.

At some point, I noticed the Maine Coon breed. It was love at first sight. The stately intellectuals captured my heart. And their easy care, beauty, and playfulness completely convinced me that it was time to acquire my first Coon.

I am very happy for the people who will be pleasantly surprised upon their first encounter with this breed. These are not just cats. These are Cat Gods. And with us, you will find worthy representatives of this breed.

Our Goal

The goal of our cattery is to breed large, vibrant, and charismatic representatives of the modern type, in accordance with the Maine Coon breed standards, with strong physical and mental health, and affectionate temperament.

This is a small home cattery, all cats are full-fledged members of our family and have free access to all areas of our home. All breeding cats in the cattery are tested for genetic diseases. Our cats are on a mixed diet, with premium class dry food available at all times. In addition, they are fed balanced meat mixes 1-2 times a day. All animals in our cattery undergo regular veterinary check-ups.
